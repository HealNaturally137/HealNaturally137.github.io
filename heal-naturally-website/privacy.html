Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-09-24

We respect your privacy. This policy describes how Heal Naturally Labs (“we”, “us”) handles information across our websites and apps.

Information we collect

  • App data (on-device): Most features work without sending data to our servers.
  • Support communications: If you email us, we keep your message to help resolve your issue.
  • Optional analytics: We may use privacy‑respecting, aggregate analytics to improve the product. You can opt out in‑app.

How we use information

  • Provide and improve our services
  • Respond to support requests
  • Meet legal obligations

Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We only share data with service providers who help us operate the app (e.g., cloud hosting) under strict confidentiality.

Data retention

We keep data only as long as necessary for the purposes described above.

Children

Our services are not directed to children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us to delete it.

Your rights

Depending on your region, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your data. Contact us at support@yourdomain.com.
